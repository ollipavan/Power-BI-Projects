## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyses sales analysis using a real-world dataset. The objective is to evaluate
Year to date sales
Year to date profit
Year to date Quantity
Year to date profit margin
Sales by Category
Sales by state
YTD Sales by region 
YTD Sales by Shipping Type
Top 5 Products by YTD Sales 
Botton 5 Products by YTD Sales
Sales by state
